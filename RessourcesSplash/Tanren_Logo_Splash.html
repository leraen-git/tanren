01 — The Mark
The Forge-Spark
A geometric mark evoking the moment of impact: the hammer striking heated steel on the anvil. The vertical axis is the hammer; the horizontal is the anvil; the radiating sparks are the work being done.

Geometric construction

The mark is built on a strict 4-axis grid. Every element is mathematically defined for perfect reproducibility at any scale.

  • Outer ring 72r · 6w
  • Inner core (steel) 14r · red
  • Cardinal bars 16w · 50h
  • Diagonal sparks 4w · red
  • Total bounding box 200×200
